# MATERNAL ai Pitch Plan

## Product Positioning

MATERNAL ai is a voice-first maternal safety net, not a generic pregnancy chatbot.

It follows a mother from positive pregnancy test through postpartum recovery, asks protocol-driven follow-up questions, detects medical and emotional risk early, and escalates structured summaries to clinicians when needed.

## Core Pitch Sentence

MATERNAL ai turns maternal follow-up into an always-on triage loop: voice check-ins, protocol-based risk detection, long-term memory, and doctor-ready escalation.

## Practical vs Psychological Framing

Pitch the product as practical medical safety infrastructure with an emotional intelligence layer.

Do not pitch it primarily as an AI therapist. That creates safety, liability, and trust issues.

The practical side gets institutional trust:

- Structured pregnancy and postpartum follow-up.
- Protocol-bound questions.
- Red/yellow/green triage.
- Doctor escalation.
- Reduced pressure on hospital staff.

The psychological side is the differentiator:

- Long-term memory across the mother journey.
- Mood and confidence trend detection.
- Early postpartum depression signal detection.
- Escalation when emotional risk becomes clinically relevant.

## The Problem

Pregnancy and postpartum care often fail between appointments.

Mothers may experience symptoms, fear, pain, bleeding, mood shifts, or recovery issues at home. Healthcare workers are overloaded, and many red flags are only caught when they become urgent.

The postpartum period is especially under-monitored. A mother may appear fine during short appointments, while gradual emotional deterioration goes unnoticed.

## The Insight

The missing layer is not another information app.

The missing layer is continuous, protocol-driven follow-up that can listen, remember, classify risk, and escalate clearly.

## The Solution

MATERNAL ai provides:

- Voice check-ins from positive test to postpartum recovery.
- A pregnancy and postpartum finite state machine.
- Protocol-bound AI responses.
- Green, yellow, and red triage.
- Long-term maternal memory.
- Doctor-ready escalation summaries.
- Early postpartum depression signal detection.

## Questions The Pitch Must Answer

### What problem are we solving?

Mothers lack continuous, structured follow-up between medical appointments, especially during postpartum recovery.

### Why now?

Voice AI, structured LLM outputs, and low-latency speech infrastructure now make it possible to create natural check-ins and safe escalation workflows.

### Why voice?

Pregnant and postpartum mothers may be tired, busy, holding a baby, or unable to type. Voice lowers friction and feels more natural during vulnerable moments.

### Why is this not just ChatGPT?

MATERNAL ai is constrained by:

- A pregnancy and postpartum finite state machine.
- A protocol retrieval layer.
- Deterministic triage rules.
- Long-term memory.
- Doctor escalation.
- Structured medical summaries.

### What is the MVP?

The hackathon MVP includes:

- Voice input.
- Transcription.
- Pregnancy/postpartum state.
- Protocol-bound LLM response.
- Green/yellow/red triage.
- Voice output.
- Doctor escalation summary.

### What is the hardest technical part?

The hardest part is not answering pregnancy questions. The hard part is safely combining longitudinal context, medical protocols, symptom extraction, and escalation logic without letting the LLM behave like an unconstrained doctor.

### What is the business or deployment model?

Possible deployment paths:

- Hospitals use it as a follow-up assistant.
- Governments fund it as maternal public health infrastructure.
- NGOs deploy it in low-access regions.
- Insurers support it to reduce late emergency interventions.
- Clinics offer it as part of maternity care packages.

## Demo Script

### Scene 1: Positive Test

User:

> I just found out I am pregnant. What should I do first?

Assistant:

> Congratulations. I can help you organize your next steps. First, I need to ask a few safety questions. Are you experiencing severe pain, heavy bleeding, fainting, or fever?

Flag:

- Green unless danger symptoms appear.

### Scene 2: Pregnancy Red Flag

User:

> I have a strong headache, my face feels swollen, and I feel dizzy.

Assistant:

> These symptoms can be serious during pregnancy. I am flagging this as urgent and preparing a summary for your care team. If symptoms are severe or you feel unsafe, contact emergency services now.

Flag:

- Red.

Doctor summary:

- Current phase.
- Symptoms.
- Severity.
- Duration.
- Known risks.
- Recommended urgency.

### Scene 3: Postpartum Mood Shift

User:

> I do not feel connected to anything. I cry every day and I am scared I am failing.

Assistant:

> I am sorry you are feeling this. You are not alone. Because this is a change from your previous check-ins and it has been happening repeatedly, I want to escalate this to a care professional. Are you having thoughts of hurting yourself or the baby?

Flag:

- Yellow or red depending on self-harm response.

## Final Pitch Narrative

MATERNAL ai is a voice-first maternal safety net for pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

It listens to mothers at home, understands where they are in the maternal journey, asks protocol-driven follow-up questions, and classifies each interaction as normal, monitor, or urgent.

When a red flag appears, it does not leave the mother alone with generic advice. It generates a structured doctor-ready summary and triggers escalation.

Its long-term memory also helps detect subtle postpartum emotional decline, making it useful for both physical safety and mental health early warning.

The goal is simple: no symptom, fear, or warning sign should disappear between appointments.
