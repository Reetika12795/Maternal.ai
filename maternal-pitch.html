Maternal AI 03 / Solution + technology partners
Our solution

A protocol-bound voice companion for the entire maternal journey.

Nine clinical states from positive test to twelve-month recovery. One short voice check-in per state, classified in real time as green, yellow, or red. Red routes straight to a clinician with a structured handoff. Every turn is auditable.
01
Protocol, not improvisation
A YAML state machine owns the conversation. The model speaks inside guardrails, not over them.
02
Voice-first by design
Mothers do not type at 4am. They speak. We meet them where they are, hands free, low friction.
03
Deterministic safety
A red-flag phrase always escalates. The model cannot downgrade a safety signal. Tested in CI.
04
Clinician handoff
Every red signal compiles a structured note: symptoms, urgency, transcript, mood trend.
